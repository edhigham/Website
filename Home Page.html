The MCU (Marvel Cinematic Universe)

02/08/2008. An important date for the MCU and Marvel fans. The MCU debuted it's franchise with Iron Man, starring Robert Downey Jr., Gwyneth Paltrow, Jon Favreau, Paul Bettany, Terrence Howard, Jeff Bridges and Clark Gregg. Since then, the MCU has become the most popular movie franchise in cinema history, grossing $17,473,727,499 worldwide in just 10 years. To date, the MCU has had 20 films (the latest one being Ant Man and the Wasp) along with the upcoming debut for Brie Larson as Captain Marvel, shortly followed by Avengers 4 and Spider-Man: Far From Home.

Iron Man

  • Director - Jon Favreau
  • Release date - 2 May 2008
  • Budget - $140,000,000
  • Box office (worldwide) - $585,174,222
  • IMDB rating - 7.9

Iron Man is the first instalment in the MCU. It tells the story of a billionaire playboy who goes from producing and selling state of the art military equipment to destroying it. After visiting Afghanistan and showing off his latest weaponry, Tony is ambushed on the way back home and captured. He must then find a way to escape his capturers and return home. From weapons manufacturer to weapons destroyer, Tony (or later known as Iron Man) must save the world from his counterpart and ex business partner, Obadiah Stane.

Trivia

  • Roughly four hundred an fifty separate pieces make up the Iron Man suit.
  • Paul Bettany (who plays J.A.R.V.I.S) recorded all of his lines in just two hours.
  • Hugh Jackman was offered the role of Tony Stark.

Captain America: The Winter Soldier

  • Director - Anthony & Joe Russo
  • Release date - 4 April 2014
  • Budget - $170,000,000
  • Box office (worldwide) - $714,264,267
  • IMDB rating - 7.8

After the events of the Avengers in New York, Steve Rogers tries to become, well, Steve Rogers. Living the normal life and adjusting to not saving the world everyday is his goal. For a superhero film we can expect this to be short lived. joining forces with our much loved Black Widow and new companion Falcon, Cap and his team set out to find out about the Winter Solider. It's only after some time that Cap realises he knows him...

Trivia

  • Anthony Mackie's appearance in this film fulfills his dream of playing a Marvel comic book character. He wrote a series of e-mail pleas to Marvel, wanting to play any comic character that would appear on film. Though the studio repeatedly replied to him that they will respond in due time, Mackie's emails caught the attention of Producer Kevin Feige, who subsequently offered him the role of Sam Wilson a.k.a. Falcon.
  • During filming, Sebastian Stan would walk around all day practicing his moves with a plastic knife because he wanted his movements to feel natural.
  • Unlike other films in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, this movie minimized the use of visual effects as much as possible. Anthony Mackie elaborated: "The Russos, what they did that was so great was, they wanted to stay with live-action, which is a dying art form. If they could build it, they built it. If we could do it, we did it. They wanted to do as little CGI as possible. That's why the movie looks so great."
